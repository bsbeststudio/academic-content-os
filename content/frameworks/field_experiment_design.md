# 实验设计回答框架

## 四要素结构

必须覆盖全部四个要素。缺任何一个都是不完整的答案。

**要素一 — 研究问题**
一句话。这个实验回答什么因果问题？

**要素二 — 处理组与控制组**
- 处理具体是什么？
- 随机化单元是什么？（个人、班级、学校）
- 这个随机化设计为什么能识别因果效应？

**要素三 — 结果变量**
- 主要结果是什么？
- 如何测量？
- 为什么这个测量对理论构念有效？

**要素四 — 威胁与局限**
至少涵盖：
- 选择偏误（随机化如何处理）
- 脱落（attrition）
- 伦理问题（欺骗或伤害如何处理）
- 外部有效性的局限

## 示例回答（英文，直接用于面试）

> "I would conduct a randomized experiment with three arms. The control group applies to universities without any AI assistance. Treatment group one has access to AI tools providing verified, accurate information. Treatment group two receives AI output that mixes correct and incorrect information—to isolate the effect of misinformation specifically.
>
> For the ethical concern: group two will receive corrected information before the end of the application process, so no participant is permanently harmed by the misinformation exposure.
>
> My primary outcome variable would be the quality of application decisions—measured by match between student qualifications and school selectivity—plus survey measures of how students attribute their outcomes to effort versus luck.
>
> The main threat to external validity is that students who volunteer for this experiment may differ from the general population. I would collect baseline characteristics and test for balance across arms."

## 常见追问

- "How would you handle the endogeneity problem?"
- "What are the threats to external validity?"
- "How would you measure your outcome variable in a scalable way?"
- "What is the ethical concern and how do you address it?"

## 准备建议

面试前针对自己的研究兴趣方向，准备好一个完整的四要素实验设计，反复练习到能在2分钟内流畅说完。这是区分强候选人和普通候选人的分水岭题目。
