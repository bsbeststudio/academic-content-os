# 未来计划与职业路径回答框架

## 核心重构

面试官不是在问"你想去哪里"。
他们在问："你来这里做什么？你会不会全力投入？"

回答重心：**对 lab 的贡献在前，个人发展在后**。
绝不能以 PhD 申请或院校品牌开头。

## 两个维度

**维度一 — 贡献（你在这里做什么）**
- 你想发展哪些技能？
- 你希望参与哪个层面？（设计、执行、分析）
- 你希望对哪类研究产出做出贡献？

**维度二 — 发展（这段经历通向哪里）**
- 这段经历如何建立你做 PhD 所需的能力？
- 说的是能力积累，不是品牌背书。

## 示例回答（英文，直接用于面试）

> "In the next two years, I want to develop two things: the technical skills to design and execute field experiments, and a substantive research contribution I can speak to in PhD applications.
>
> Concretely, I hope to be involved in at least one project from design through analysis—not just the data work but understanding the research decisions at each stage. That's the kind of training I can't get from coursework alone.
>
> After this predoc, I plan to apply to PhD programs in economics with a focus on education and behavioral economics. This lab is specifically valuable because the research agenda maps directly onto the questions I want to pursue."

## 常见追问

- "Why a PhD specifically?"
- "What field within economics?"
- "Have you thought about which programs?"
