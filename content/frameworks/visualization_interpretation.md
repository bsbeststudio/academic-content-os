# 数据可视化解读框架

## 三层解读结构

开口第一句：

> "Let me interpret at three levels."

**第一层 — 描述 pattern（30秒）**
说出图中最striking的一个发现。
不要描述图的外观或坐标轴，直接说发现。

**第二层 — 连接回归结果（45秒）**
解释图如何补充或解释回归系数。
图揭示的是回归系数所概括的机制或分布。

**第三层 — 政策结论（20秒）**
对会议受众或非技术受众的核心信息。
必须超越"treatment has an effect"这个层面。

## 示例回答（英文，直接用于面试）

> "Let me interpret at three levels. Descriptively, the most striking pattern is that Preschool children are much more likely to be egalitarian—41.7% compared to 31% in the control group—while Parent Academy children are actually less likely to be egalitarian than control. That's an interesting divergence between two intervention groups.
>
> Connecting to the regression: Table 3 shows Preschool significantly reduces inequality in luck-based experiments. This figure explains the mechanism—the treatment shifted children's underlying fairness type, not just their choices in one experiment.
>
> For a conference audience, the key takeaway is that early education shapes moral preferences, not just skills. And the contrast between Preschool and Parent Academy suggests that how and where children are educated matters—these two interventions pushed fairness preferences in opposite directions.
>
> One limitation: my figure doesn't show statistical significance markers. In a revised version I would add those to make the inferential story clearer."

## 常见追问

- "How do you interpret this result together with the main result?"
- "What is the takeaway for the audience?"
- "Why did you choose this type of visualization over alternatives?"
- "What would you add if you were to revise this figure?"

## 主动承认局限

主动说出图的不足是加分项，体现自我审视能力。
承认不足后必须立即说明如何改进，不能只停在承认。
