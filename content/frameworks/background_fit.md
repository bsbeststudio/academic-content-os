# 背景匹配度回答框架

## 核心重构

不要试图证明自己与岗位完美匹配。
要证明的是：你的背景与岗位需求**互补**。

Predoc 本来就是学习岗位。说"我来这里学 X"不是弱点，是对 predoc 定位的正确理解。

## 三步结构

**第一步 — 直接承认差异**
> "You're right that I haven't done X before—I want to be upfront about that."

诚实建立信任。不要跳过这一步。

**第二步 — 转化真实可迁移技能**
三类常见可迁移优势（观测数据/中国背景）：
1. 大规模数据处理能力：数据清洗、合并、面板数据构建
2. 领域实质性知识：对教育政策机制的理解（有助于实验设计和结果解读）
3. Coding exercise 本身就是 RCT 数据分析的证明（处理组分配、balance check）

**第三步 — 明确说出学习计划**
点名具体的 gap，说这正是这个岗位要学的内容。
> "I see that as exactly what I should be learning in this role, and I'd make it a priority from day one."

## 示例回答（英文，直接用于面试）

> "You're right that my background is observational data, and I haven't run a field experiment before—I want to be upfront about that. But I think what I bring is genuinely complementary.
>
> On the technical side, working with large-scale administrative datasets has given me strong experience handling messy data, constructing variables, and running panel regressions—the downstream analysis that experimental data also requires. On the substantive side, I have real domain knowledge about how education systems shape behavior, which helps with both experiment design and interpretation. And the coding exercise itself was RCT data—understanding treatment assignment and balance checks was central to replicating those tables correctly.
>
> The gap I genuinely have is the operational side—recruitment, IRB, field coordination. I see that as exactly what I should be learning in this role, and I'd make it a priority from day one."
